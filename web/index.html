Research Workspace

Explainable AI research with paper-trading execution.

The workspace combines live Alpaca paper-account data, market research, price history, news, and a human-confirmed trade workflow in one deployable UI.

Execution mode Paper only

All orders are routed through Alpaca's paper-trading environment.

01

Account Context

The dashboard reads cash, buying power, portfolio value, positions, and recent orders directly from the connected Alpaca paper account.

02

Research Flow

Enter a ticker to fetch recent daily bars and related news, then generate a compact stock brief with trend indicators, risks, and catalysts.

03

Trade Planning

The app previews entry, stop, target, estimated position size, confidence, and reasons before any order button becomes available.

04

Human Approval

Orders are submitted only after a separate confirmation step, keeping the assistant in a research-and-decision-support role.

How It Works

  • Connect: Alpaca paper credentials load securely from environment variables.
  • Observe: The app reads account status, current positions, and order history.
  • Research: Ticker data is converted into a brief, chart, indicators, news, and risks.
  • Preview: A trade plan is generated separately from execution.
  • Execute: Paper orders require an explicit user confirmation.
